Advanced Features

🧠

AI Decision Engine

Powered by 60+ AI models via 9Router with automatic fallback. Uses GPT-4, Claude, and local LLMs for intelligent trading decisions with self-reflection and learning memory.

🎯

Dynamic Position Sizing

Kelly Criterion + Volatility-based lot calculation. Automatically adjusts position size based on win rate, ATR, and account equity for optimal risk management.

📊

DXY Correlation Tracking

Monitor Gold-Dollar correlation in real-time. Detects divergence patterns and blocks trades during anomalous market conditions for fundamental confirmation.

🛡️

Advanced Risk Management

Multi-layer protection: drawdown limits, confidence thresholds, circuit breakers, directional conflict detection, and spread validation.

📈

Trailing Stop Manager

ATR-based adaptive trailing stop with breakeven automation. Locks profit at 2R, aggressive trail at 3R, and dynamic spread-aware breakeven.

🔍

Performance Tracker

Real-time monitoring vs backtest expectations. Alerts on win rate deviation, profit factor drops, and excessive drawdown with statistical significance checks.

🗳️

AI Ensemble Voting

Multiple AI models vote on each decision. Reduces hallucination and bias through majority consensus with configurable agreement threshold.

📚

Learning Memory

Self-reflection after every trade. AI learns from wins and losses, building a weighted memory of market patterns and trading lessons.

🎨

Enhanced Technical Analysis

Support/Resistance detection, Price Action patterns (HH/HL, LH/LL), Market Structure analysis, and multi-timeframe trend confirmation.
